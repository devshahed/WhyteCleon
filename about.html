Our History

WhyteCleon Limited was incorporated in 2010 and commenced full operation immediately. WhyteCleon leverages big data gathered from market intelligence and historical records in its decision making, strategy formation, product development, relationship and employee management. Our wealth of experience has positioned us to deliver improved and strategic value added services to our clients’ corporate objectives.
